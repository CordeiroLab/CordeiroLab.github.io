## Biography

>[**Aamina Sahar**](https://aaminasahar.com) - I am a biomolecular researcher interested in understanding how biomolecules regulate cellular function and disease. I joined the Cordeiro Lab at ITQB NOVA in 2026 as a BRIDGE Fellow. My current work focuses on developing biomolecular systems for phenylketonuria (PKU), a metabolic disorder associated with elevated phenylalanine levels. Through this project, I am gaining experience in recombinant protein production, protein purification, and the biochemical characterization of engineered biomolecular systems. My research aims to contribute to the development of responsive biomolecular platforms with potential applications in disease monitoring and therapeutic intervention.

## Research Interests
